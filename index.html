Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like to delete your data from our app, please follow these steps:

Method 1: Delete Through Facebook

  1. Go to your Facebook Account Settings
  2. Click on "Apps and Websites"
  3. Find our app in the list
  4. Click "Remove" to revoke access and delete your data
